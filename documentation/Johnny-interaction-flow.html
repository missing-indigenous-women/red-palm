Interaction Flow

Precondition: Johnny is about to open the MMIW web app for the first time.

Post-condition: Johnny has connected with other users of the app and are actively sharing new info in realtime.

  1. Johnny opens app and sees the case he has interest in.
  2. johnny clicks on his case and a picture with vital info shows up. Including a location on a map.
  3. Johnny reviews the info and it all looks up to date.
  4. Johnny sees that 20 junior internet sleuths are on the case.
  5. He has more info he would like to share with the sleuths.
  6. Johnny clicks the case board or case forum to share that info
  7. Johnny enters board and posts his new info after typing and pressing the submit button.
  8. All sleuths involved see the new message and votes on the relevance of that info so as not to throw other sleuths
    off.
